Toilet Cleaner

Toilet Cleaner

Fast-acting, perfumed toilet cleaner and descaler. Clings to vertical surfaces, removes heavy soil and limescale.

Benefits


  • Fast & thorough removal of limescale & stains
  • Extended contact time improves cleaning
  • Fresh fragrance

Applications

  • Cold weather applications
  • Fast track construction
  • Reduced construction time
  • Enhanced performance

Usage Instruction

  • Use undiluted directly from the bottle.
  • Squeeze under the rim and around the bowl.
  • Scrub with a toilet brush.
  • Let sit for 5 minutes.
  • Flush to rinse.
Bathroom Cleaner

Bathroom Cleaner

Thick formula cleaner for floors, tiles, taps, sinks, ceramic and steel surfaces.

Benefits


  • Fresh fragrance
  • Better than bleach, acid & phenyl
  • Safe for all bathroom fittings

Applications

  • Regular Cleaning: 1.5 cap in 4L water
  • Tough Stains: Use undiluted

Usage Instruction


  • Regular Cleaning: Mix 1.5 caps in 4L water and clean.
  • Tough Stains: Apply undiluted directly.
  • Disinfection: Dilute, apply on surface, wait 5 minutes, scrub and rinse.
Hygienic Hard Surface Cleaner

Hygienic Hard Surface Cleaner

For hygienic cleaning of hard, water-resistant surfaces.

Benefits


  • Hygienically clean
  • Removes oil & residue
  • Doesn't damage surfaces

Applications

  • Small Area: Spray on damp cloth, clean, rinse
  • Large Surface: 1 pump per 5L water, mop, rinse, dry

Usage Instruction


  • Small Area: Spray on a damp cloth, clean, then rinse.
  • Large Surface: Mix 1 pump in 5L water, mop surface, rinse, and dry.
Floor Cleaner

Floor Cleaner

Heavy-duty, multi-surface cleaner for floors.

Benefits


  • Doesn't damage floors
  • Prevents scale buildup
  • Fast, hygienic results

Applications

  • Small Area: Use undiluted
  • Large Surface: Dilute 10% in water

Usage Instruction


  • Small Area: Use undiluted for spot cleaning.
  • Large Surface: Dilute 10% in water, mop the surface, rinse and buff dry.
